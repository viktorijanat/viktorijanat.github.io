Unit Six

I was working for a company that makes technology products. I have been asked to produce two graphic products to be used in a promotional campaign for a new smartphone. The promotional campaign will include the following. A user guide – containing a line drawing(s) of the new smartphone illustrating the size and main features. An advert – containing an image(s) of the smartphone in use and compressed appropriately for viewing on screen (i.e. on the website used to advertise the smartphone) and on paper (i.e. in a high-end magazine).
