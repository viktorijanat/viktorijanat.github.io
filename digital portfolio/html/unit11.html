Unit Eleven

A new ‘client’ wants me to design and develop a small computer network for their office in a nearby town. There is currently a team of eight people working in the office. These people are all using stand-alone computer systems, one of which is connected to a printer. I have also been asked to consider the costs involved with setting up a network, in terms of the hardware components and software resources. The network should also be set up to have additional capacity as the business grows. Including set of user requirements which i had to follow.
